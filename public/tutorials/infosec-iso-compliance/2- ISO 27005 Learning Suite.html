ISO 27005 and ISO 27001 — The Relationship
ISO 27001 clauses 6.1.2 and 8.2 require a risk assessment process — ISO/IEC 27005 provides the methodology to fulfil that requirement. It is not a certification standard itself but the operational risk management engine behind a conformant ISMS. The 2022 revision aligns closely with ISO 31000 terminology and introduces explicit scenario-based and asset-based approaches.
