title: "Pexformer: Robust Indoor Human Localisation via Patch-level Tokenisation and Semi-Permeable Attention"
authors: ["B. Zhang", "S. Rafique", "Mohamad Reza Shahabian Alashti", "Shadiya Alingal Meethal", "V. Velmurugan", "Patrick Holthaus", "et al."]
venue: "ICSR 2026"
year: "2026"
abstract: "Pexformer is a transformer-based approach to robust indoor human localisation. It introduces patch-level tokenisation and a semi-permeable attention mechanism to improve robustness in cluttered indoor environments relevant to assisted living and social robotics. The method targets reliable spatial awareness for robots operating alongside people in the home."
