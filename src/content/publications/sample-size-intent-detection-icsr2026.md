abstract: "This paper provides practical sample-size guidance for multimodal intent-detection studies in assistive wearable robotics, helping researchers move from small pilot datasets to well-powered data-collection protocols. Using HD-EMG and IMU data, it offers guidance on how much data is needed to obtain reliable model evaluation, supporting reproducible study design in wearable assistive systems."
