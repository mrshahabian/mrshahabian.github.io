title: "EMG-Based Lower Limb Activity Recognition for Exoskeleton-Assisted and Unassisted Locomotion Using Deep Learning"
authors: ["Shadiya Alingal Meethal", "Mohamad Reza Shahabian Alashti", "M. Mosso", "M. Sartori", "M. I. Refai", "I. Pacifico", "et al."]
venue: "ICSR 2026"
year: "2026"
abstract: "This study applies deep learning to surface-EMG signals for recognising lower-limb locomotion activities under both exoskeleton-assisted and unassisted conditions. We examine how assistance affects muscle-activation patterns and recognition performance, and evaluate models intended for real-time intent detection in wearable assistive robotics. The findings inform the design of adaptive control for exosuits and exoskeletons."

Part of the SWAG (Soft Wearable Assistive Garment) research programme on biosignal-driven intent detection for assistive wearable robotics.
