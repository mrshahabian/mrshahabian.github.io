## Full-Day Workshop on Agentic AI

A **full-day workshop on Agentic AI** at the University of Hertfordshire on the **18th of April 2026** — a day truly full of agentic AI.

It was inspiring to see **55 highly talented students** join us in person, alongside an incredible **140 hours of online engagement**. What a truly wonderful day it was — full of energy, ideas, and meaningful progress.

### About the Workshop

Dive into the world of Agentic AI and discover how to design, build, and deploy intelligent AI agents capable of autonomous decision-making. This workshop takes you step-by-step through the foundations of agentic systems, including architecture design, Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), and real-world applications.

Through live demonstrations and practical insights, you'll learn how modern AI agents are built and applied across different domains. Whether you're a developer, AI enthusiast, or tech innovator, this session gives you hands-on experience and a deeper understanding of agentic AI.

---

### What You Will Learn

- What Agentic AI is and how it differs from traditional AI
- Core components of AI agents: LLMs, tools, memory, orchestration
- Introduction to RAG systems and agent architectures
- Practical examples and live coding demonstrations
